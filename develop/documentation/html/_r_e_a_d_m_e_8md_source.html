1 # LMPEP - Laguerre's Method Applied to the Polynomial Eigenvalue Problem ##
2 LMPEP is a Fortran library collection of solvers and their dependencies for the polynomial eigenvalue problem. There are several subroutines that make use of external subroutines from the BLAS and LAPACK libraries.
